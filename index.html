In December 2025, the US Treasury lifted sanctions on Syria. The EU followed. But major tech platforms — ChatGPT, Figma, PayPal, Slack, GitLab, and more — continue to block Syrian users through inertia, not policy.

Syrians rebuilding their country can't access the tools the rest of the world takes for granted. Click for Syria intercepts those blocked pages and turns the moment of frustration into direct advocacy — a one-click path to the decision-makers who can actually change this.
